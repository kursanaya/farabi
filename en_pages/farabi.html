1155-TH ANNIVERSARY OF AL-FARABI

DEDICATED TO THE 1155TH ANNIVERSARY OF THE GREAT THINKER

2025 marks the 1155th anniversary of the birth of the great scientist, philosopher, mathematician and musician al-Farabi. His works had a huge impact on the development of science, education and culture not only in the East, but throughout the world.

In honor of this significant date, an international forum is being held to discuss Farabi's works, his contribution to the development of philosophy, ethics, pedagogy and modern science.
